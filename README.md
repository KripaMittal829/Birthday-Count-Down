
The **Birthday Countdown** is a delightful, pastel‑pink themed experience designed to build excitement for a special day.  

- 🎀 **Countdown Page** — softly styled in pastel pink, showing the time left until the birthday.  
- 💌 **Two Waiting Pages** — filled with cute, heartwarming text to keep anticipation alive.  
- 🌟 **Lights On Page** — floating emojis drift across the screen, adding a whimsical touch.  
- 🎶 **Music Page** — a “Play Music” button starts a gentle background tune for cozy vibes.  
- 🎂 **Final Celebration Page** — features a birthday cake and a heartfelt wish to mark the big moment.  

With its soft pastel pinkish shades, playful animations, and gentle music, this webapp creates a warm, joyful, and memorable way to celebrate birthdays online.

## 🚀 Features
- Pastel pink UI with calming aesthetic  
- Interactive countdown timer  
- Cute waiting pages with themed text  
- Floating emoji animations  
- Background music toggle  
- Final cake + birthday wish page
